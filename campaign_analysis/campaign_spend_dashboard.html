Executive Summary

Campaign spend has shown significant growth from January 2024 to Q1 2025, with total monthly spend more than doubling from $144,989 to approximately $300,000 per month. This substantial increase indicates an expanded marketing strategy and increased investment in digital user acquisition.

The campaign portfolio has evolved over time, with a shift from affiliate-dominated campaigns in January 2024 to UberEats campaigns leading the spend in 2025. UberEats Android Canada has emerged as the consistent top performer in both February and March 2025, replacing the US-focused affiliate campaigns that led in early 2024.

Budget allocation has become more balanced across campaigns, with the top campaign representing a smaller percentage of total spend in 2025 (16-19%) compared to January 2024 (27.3%). This suggests a more diversified approach to campaign investment and risk management.

Regional investment has expanded significantly, with substantial spend across North America (US, Canada, Mexico), Europe (UK, France, Spain), and emerging markets (India, Brazil, Argentina). March 2025 has seen the introduction of a new Taiwan campaign, indicating further expansion into Asian markets.

Month-to-month consistency in Q1 2025 shows strategic stability, with the top 5 campaigns remaining largely unchanged between February and March. This suggests that the current campaign strategy is delivering reliable results and has been optimized based on performance data.

Based on the current trajectory, March 2025 is projected to slightly outperform February with an estimated total spend of $309,000, representing a 4.7% month-over-month increase. This continued growth indicates strong campaign performance and ongoing commitment to digital marketing channels.
